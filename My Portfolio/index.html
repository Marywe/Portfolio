About me

Hello, World! My name is María "Marywe" López, and I'm a passionate videogame developer.

My primary role in the industry —and the one I'm most passionate about— is Quality Assurance (QA). I've worked on several projects as a QA, but I also consider myself versatile, as I have contributed to other projects through design, coding, and even modeling. Currently, my three-member team and I are working on creating a board game!

Additionally, I hold a certificate in Data Analysis, which I hope to leverage to enhance my professional performance. Moreover, I would love to explore new opportunities as a QA Analyst or Data Analyst within the video game industry. You cand find my final project in Kaggle.

Playing video games has been my favorite hobby for as long as I can remember. Since discovering the first video game degree programs I have devoted myself to this field, investing significant time and effort to turn my passion into a career. Every day, I strive to become a better developer. I continuously learn new techniques and take on small projects to grow and evolve. Video game development is an incredibly dynamic field with endless possibilities, and I'm proud of what I've achieved so far and excited for what lies ahead.

Projects

As part of a small team, each of us managed a wide variety of tasks, including: localization, compatibility, security, regression, functionality, performance, audiovisual and certification tests. The tests were executed both on PC and console builds, as well as within the game engine.
